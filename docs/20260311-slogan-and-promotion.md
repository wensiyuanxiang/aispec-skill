## 宣传重点 | Key selling points

- **主流框架全覆盖**：Next.js、Vue/Nuxt、Flutter、Go、Java、Python、Node、Rust、Tauri、Qt、CLI、AI/LLM 等各大主流开发系统与框架的最佳实践项目规范，一库搞定。
- **大厂级架构**：规约对齐成熟代码库与软件架构最佳实践，让代码符合大厂级工程规范。
- **团队协同**：统一规范便于不同成员、多团队的项目管理与协同，减少风格漂移，便于评审与交接。
- **新手即享顶级架构**：新成员、新项目一句话获得完整规约，无需资深架构师搭架子，新手也能从一开始就拥有顶级架构。

- **Mainstream stacks covered**: Best-practice project specs for Next.js, Vue/Nuxt, Flutter, Go, Java, Python, Node, Rust, Tauri, Qt, CLI, AI/LLM, and more—one repo, all major frameworks.
- **Enterprise-grade**: Specs align with mature codebase and software-architecture best practices.
- **Team collaboration**: One set of norms for members and teams; easier project management, review, and handoff.
- **Top-tier structure for everyone**: New joiners and new projects get a full spec in one prompt; no senior architect required—newcomers get top-tier architecture from day one.
